Portal Electrónico de Documentos Personales del Estado

Este portal servirá como una plataforma centralizada donde estarán disponibles todos los documentos personales importantes relacionados con el Estado o servicios importantes. A través de este sistema, los ciudadanos podrán acceder de manera segura, rápida y organizada a su información oficial, como certificados, identificaciones, registros y otros documentos relevantes.

Key Features

Document Upload

Easily upload your personal documents, such as IDs, contracts, or certificates, with a simple drag-and-drop interface. Supports multiple file formats for your convenience.

Instant Sharing

Share your documents securely with others via temporary links or direct access, with options to set permissions and expiration dates for enhanced control.

Fast Search

Find any document quickly using our advanced search functionality. Search by keywords, tags, or categories to locate your files in seconds.

Activity Tracking

Monitor document activity, including uploads, downloads, and shares, with a detailed log to keep track of who accessed your files and when.
