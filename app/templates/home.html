Key Features

Document Upload

Easily upload your personal documents, such as IDs, contracts, or certificates, using a simple drag-and-drop interface. Multiple file formats supported for your convenience.

Instant Sharing

Share your documents securely with others via temporary links or direct access. Set permissions and expiration dates for enhanced control.

Fast Search

Quickly find any document using our advanced search feature. Search by keywords, tags, or categories.

Activity Tracking

Monitor all activity such as uploads, downloads, and shares. A detailed log keeps track of who accessed your documents and when.
